About Me

Welcome! I am a PhD student in Economics at Cornell University. My research focuses on understanding and improving educational outcomes while considering their broader implications for labor markets and policy.

Research Interests:

  • Economics of Education
  • Labor Economics
  • Policy Analysis

I am particularly interested in studying how educational policies and interventions affect student outcomes and later life success. My research combines empirical analysis with policy-relevant questions to contribute to our understanding of education and labor markets.
